## 📌 Overview

SkillShare Hub is a web application designed to bridge the gap between education and industry by connecting students and freshers with experienced professionals.

Many students struggle to find the right career path due to scattered resources and lack of guidance. SkillShare Hub provides verified mentorship, structured career roadmaps, discussion forums, and curated learning resources in one place.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

- Lack of career guidance
- Information overload
- Gap between academics and industry
- No centralized mentorship platform

---

## 💡 Solution

SkillShare Hub provides:

- ✅ Verified Mentors
- ✅ Career Roadmaps
- ✅ Skill-based Search
- ✅ Discussion Forum
- ✅ Resource Library
- ✅ Personalized Feed
